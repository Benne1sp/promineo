Unsure of what class to play in WoW?

Well I am here to help!

There are 3 roles in the game:

Tank

In World of Warcraft, a tank is a character role responsible for absorbing damage and protecting other members of a group or raid. Tanks draw the attention of enemies using abilities that generate threat. Tanks have high health pools, strong defenses, and abilities to control enemy movement or reduce the damage taken, allowing the rest of the team to focus on dealing damage. Their primary job is to survive while managing threat and positioning enemies for optimal attacks from the team.

Healer

In World of Warcraft, a healer is a character role focused on restoring health to allies during combat. Healers use spells and abilities to heal damage taken by group members, ensuring they stay alive throughout encounters. Healers need to manage their resources, prioritize targets, and react quickly to shifting situations, balancing healing output with positioning to avoid taking damage themselves. Their primary goal is to keep the team alive and ensure they can continue to fight.

DPS

In World of Warcraft, a DPS, broken into melee and ranged, is a character role focused on dealing damage to enemies. DPS characters aim to output as much damage as possible to defeat enemies quickly. They have abilities designed to maximize their damage, while also managing resources. DPS players typically focus on avoiding damage and positioning themselves optimally to contribute to the success of the group or raid. Their primary role is to eliminate threats as efficiently as possible.

You will find on the "Spec" page of this project a list of each different Class in the game, along with their 3 or 4 different specs they can play, and what role within the game that spec can fill.
